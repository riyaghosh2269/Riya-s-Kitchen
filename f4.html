What is Sada Vat??????

Shada Bhat, meaning plain white rice, is a staple in Bengali households and an essential part of daily meals. It is usually made with parboiled or aromatic rice, cooked until soft and fluffy. Though simple, it serves as the perfect base for a variety of Bengali dishes. Shada bhat pairs beautifully with lentils (dal), vegetables, fish, or meat curries. It allows the bold flavors of mustard-based or spicy gravies to shine through. In Bengali culture, meals often begin with shada bhat and a light vegetable or bitter dish. It is also a comforting food, associated with home-cooked warmth and simplicity. Ghee or a squeeze of lemon is sometimes added for a subtle richness.
